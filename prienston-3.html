In The News

D1 Training Featured in The Wall Street Journal

D1 Training, featured in a cover story of The Wall Street Journal, showcases the success of Vic Viktorov, owner of a Boston-area franchise, in adopting AI-driven tools. By transforming key operations such as marketing and HR, Viktorov has streamlined processes, leading to a 30% revenue increase and demonstrating the power of technology in driving business growth.
