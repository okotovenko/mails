Princeton Equity Group is proud to bring its expertise in scaling franchisor and multi-location concepts to Barry’s

Capital and Expertise for Leading Franchisor & Multi-Location Businesses.

.We are excited to share that Princeton Equity Group has partnered with Barry’s, the global leader in boutique fitness.

Known for their high-energy, results-driven workouts and vibrant community spirit, Barry’s has been transforming lives since 1998. With 89 studios worldwide and over 7 million visits in 2024, Barry’s has established itself as a cultural phenomenon. Our strategic investment will enable Barry’s to expand its unparalleled fitness experience to new markets, including further growth in the UK and Canada. This exemplifies the Princeton track record through continued growth of franchisor and multi-location concepts.
