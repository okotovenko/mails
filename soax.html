Smart Routing rolling out to all users

Smart Routing autonomously manages the load and requests across multiple exit nodes, significantly reducing the chance of hitting resource limits or similar constraints. After testing this feature with a few selected customers, it has now been rolled out to everyone. There's no action needed on your end, you're already benefiting from this update.
